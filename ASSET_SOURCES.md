## Platform collection workflow

For images discovered on Instagram, Xiaohongshu, Pinterest, TikTok, brand sites, or creator media accounts:

1. Save the URL, creator handle, platform, post date, and intended inspiration category.
2. Do not rehost the image on the public site until permission or a license is confirmed.
3. Use the link internally as a mood-board reference.
4. Replace public gallery images with owned shoots, commissioned creator images, or signed partner content.
